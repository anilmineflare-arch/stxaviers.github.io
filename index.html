Our Story

Learning Beyond the Classroom

Founded in 2015, Paramanad School has nurtured over 12,000 graduates — doctors, engineers, artists, and changemakers — through a philosophy that honours both intellect and imagination.

  • 🔬
    STEM Labs — State-of-the-art science, robotics, and computer labs equipped for 21st-century learning.
  • 🎨
    Arts & Culture — Dedicated studios for visual art, music, drama, and classical dance.
  • 🏆
    Sports Excellence — Olympic-size pool, cricket ground, basketball courts, and trained coaches.
  • 💡
    Innovation Hub — Maker space and incubator for student projects and entrepreneurial thinking.
